COQ AU RIESLING

Serves: 2

Time: 1 1/2 hours

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/4 lb of bacon, chopped into 1 inch pieces
  • 2-4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1/2 medium sweet onion, diced
  • 2 oz button mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup Italian parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh tarragon
  • 1/2 bottle of dry riesling wine
  • salt (to taste)
  • pepper (to taste)

RECIPE PREPARATION

In a Dutch oven, starting from cold, sauté the bacon until the fat has rendered, then add the onion. Cook for a few minutes more, until onion is soft and golden. Remove and place on plate. Reserve for later. Leave some fat in the pan.

Season the chicken liberally with salt and pepper.

Place the chicken, skin side down, into the hot pan for about 5 minutes on each side, until both sides are thoroughly browned. Remove to a plate when finished.

Sauté mushrooms, until golden. Add back in the chicken, bacon and onion and situate comfortably in the pan.

Add the wine, then bring to a boil. Partially cover and simmer over low heat for 1 hour.

Sprinkle the dish with parsley and tarragon before serving.
