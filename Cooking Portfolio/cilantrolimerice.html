CILANTRO LIME RICE

Serves: 2

Time: 20 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 3/4 cup long grain white rice
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • zest of one lime
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tsp kosher salt

RECIPE PREPARATION

Add the rice, water and bay leaf to a pot and bring to a boil over high heat. Once boiling, reduce heat to low and cover with the lid as it simmers for 15 minutes.

Open the lid and fluff the rice, then replace the cover for 5 more minutes to absorb the steam.

Remember to remove the bay leaf, then toss the rice with lime juice, lime zest, cilantro and salt.
