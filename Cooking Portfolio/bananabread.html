BANANA BREAD

Makes: 2 loaves

Time: 1 hour & 45 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 3-4 medium overripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 2/3 cup sugar
  • 2/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup chocolate chips (optional)

RECIPE PREPARATION

Preheat oven to 350ÂºF. Grease two 9-inch loaf pans with butter or non-stick spray.

In a mixing bowl, thoroughly combine the bananas, sugar, oil and vanilla. Add in the eggs, one at a time, continuing to stir until well-incorporated. There will be a few lumps in the batter from the bananas!

In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt and cinnamon. Gradually add this mixture to the wet mixture, pouring in a little at a time then stirring until finished. Add in chocolate chips here, if desired.

Separate the batter evenly into the two greased pans, then place in the oven for an hour. Test them loaves with a toothpick to make sure it is baked through. Let rest for at least 15 minutes before slicing. Cover with plastic wrap to preserve for up to 5 days.
