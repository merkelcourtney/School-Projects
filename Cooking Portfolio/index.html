About the recipes

How in the world the recipes came about

Pictures of me cooking can be found at as early as 2 years old. Ever since then, I've become my mother's regular kitchen nuisance. I love to experiment with everything food-related â€“ most of the time related to a Facebook recipe video a family member shared to my wall. While that is only partially a joke, I got tired of making recipes on Pinterest that never quite met my expectations, or measured up to the 5 star ratings they were given. So, that's how my recipe development began.

I start with an idea of how something is generally made, then tweak aspects of it based on the vast cooking knowledge I can mostly attribute to the Internet. I aim to be simplistic, but produce the highest quality food an at-home cook can do. Every recipe is designed with that in mind! I hope these recipes serve you well; I sure think they are 5-star rated.
