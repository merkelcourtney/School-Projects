NO-KNEAD CRUSTY BREAD

Serves: 8

Time: 1 day

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm filtered water

RECIPE PREPARATION

Measure out 1 1/2 cups of filtered water, and microwave for 15-30 seconds, until water is just barely warmer than room temperature. Stir in honey until dissolved. Add the active dry yeast, stir, then set aside until foamy. This usually takes between 5 and 10 minutes.

Whisk together flour and salt in a large bowl. Pour in the honey, water & yeast mixture. Stir to combine using a wooden spoon. Make sure all the flour has been incorporated into the wet shaggy mixture.

Cover with plastic wrap and let rest for 18-24 hours. Turn out onto a floured piece of parchment. Form into a ball as best you can (it will still be wet and shaggy), by slightly kneading – this entails folding it a couple of times. Dust with flour, then cover with plastic wrap and let rest on the counter for 2 hours.

Preheat large dutch oven in the oven at 450º for at least 30 minutes.

Once the dough springs back when pressed, lift the parchment paper with the dough into the preheated dutch oven. Cover with lid, and bake for 30 minutes at 450º. Remove the lid and bake for another 15 minutes uncovered. Once done, lift the parchment onto a wire rack to cool for at least 2 hours before slicing.

Store in a brown paper bag or under a linen towel for 2-3 days.
